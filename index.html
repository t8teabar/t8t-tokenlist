Polygon Utility Token T8 Ecosystem

Project Overview

T8T is a utility token deployed on the Polygon network and integrated into the T8 Tea Bar ecosystem. It is designed exclusively for non-financial purposes such as loyalty rewards, predefined product redemption, and community engagement within a controlled environment.

T8T does not represent equity, investment, dividends, profit-sharing, or any financial instrument.
