T8T Utility Token Whitepaper
(Non-Financial Use – Version 1.0)
1. Introduction

This document describes the technical design, functional scope, and intended non-financial use of the T8 / T8T Token (the “Token”).

This whitepaper is provided solely for informational, technical, and operational reference purposes.
It does not constitute an offer, solicitation, recommendation, or invitation to purchase, invest in, trade, or otherwise acquire any financial instrument, security, or investment product.

The Token is designed strictly as a utility mechanism within specific closed and controlled application environments operated under the T8 ecosystem.

2. Project Background

The T8 project originates from the operation of physical beverage retail environments, where digital tools are used to enhance customer interaction, engagement, and experience.

The T8 / T8T Token is developed as part of an internal digital utility framework supporting:

Loyalty-style point interactions

Controlled redemption of predefined goods

Community engagement and recognition features

Digital participation experiences linked to physical locations

The project does not aim to create a financial product, investment platform, or speculative asset.

3. Token Nature and Current Positioning

The Token is implemented and positioned as a non-financial utility token

The Token does not confer:

The Token is not intended to function as:

A financial instrument

4. Intended Use Cases (Current Scope)

Within its current operational scope, the Token may be used exclusively for the following non-financial utility purposes:

4.1 Loyalty and Points-Based Interaction

Recording user participation within T8-operated environments

Internal points-style mechanisms associated with store activities

Non-transferable or controlled exchanges within closed systems

4.2 Physical Goods Redemption

Exchange of Tokens for predefined physical goods, such as:

Beverages offered at T8 retail locations

Redemption values and rules are set internally and may vary by location or campaign

4.3 Community Interaction and “Lucky Wall” Features

Submission of images, text messages, or short video content

Display of approved content on in-store digital screens (“Lucky Wall”)

Recognition and engagement experiences that are non-financial in nature

All submitted content is subject to moderation and approval mechanisms to prevent misuse, inappropriate material, or abuse.

4.4 Digital Experiences

Participation in non-financial digital experiences

Access to visual recognition, acknowledgments, or interactive displays

Engagement-driven features without monetary value

All use cases operate within closed or semi-closed environments designated by the operating entities.

5. Content Moderation and Operational Controls

To maintain operational integrity and user safety:

All user-generated content (images, text, videos) is subject to review

Automated and manual moderation mechanisms may be applied

Content that fails moderation criteria may be rejected or removed

No entitlement or guarantee is associated with content display

The Token does not grant any right to bypass moderation or enforcement processes.
